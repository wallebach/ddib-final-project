Subscription Platform

The Subscription Platform is a decentralized application that revolutionizes content monetization through blockchain technology. Built on Ethereum and Avalanche networks, it enables creators to monetize their content through subscription models while giving users governance rights through token staking.

Our platform leverages smart contracts to ensure transparent, trustless transactions between creators and subscribers. The dual-token system (STR for subscriptions, GRN for governance) creates a sustainable ecosystem where stakeholders can participate in platform decision-making while enjoying premium content.

Key features include creator registration, flexible subscription pricing, token staking rewards, democratic governance, and multi-network deployment capabilities. The platform is designed to be scalable, secure, and user-friendly.
